"The Honest Woodcutter"
Once upon a time, a poor woodcutter was cutting wood near a river. Suddenly, his axe slipped from his hands and fell into the water. He was very sad because it was his only axe. Then, a fairy appeared and asked what happened. The woodcutter told the truth. The fairy showed him a golden axe, but he said it was not his. Then she showed him a silver axe, and again he said no. Finally, she showed his old axe. The woodcutter was happy and said it was his. The fairy gave him all three axes because he was honest.
